# 📊 Ledger Finance Tracker & Kindred Management

A collection of sleek, modern, and lightweight web tools designed to simplify your daily life, finances, and relationships.

---

## 💵 Ledger Finance Tracker

A sleek, modern, and lightweight personal finance management tool built to track your daily income, expenses, and overall budget in real-time. 

🚀 **[View Live Demo](https://khaleelsyed8.github.io/NewLife/Ledger_Finance_Tracker.html)**

### ✨ Features

* **Real-Time Balance Tracking:** Instantly calculates your current balance, total income, and total expenses.
* **Dynamic Ledger:** Add, view, and organize financial transactions on the fly.
* **Clean UI/UX:** A minimalist, distraction-free interface designed for seamless data entry and modern aesthetics.
* **Fully Responsive:** Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile screens so you can track your budget on the go.
* **Static & Fast:** Pure frontend implementation ensuring lightning-fast load times.

---

## ❤️ Kindred Relationship Management

A personal CRM-style platform designed to help you nurture, track, and strengthen your relationships with friends, family, and professional connections.

🚀 **[View Live Demo](https://khaleelsyed8.github.io/NewLife/Kindred.html)**

### ✨ Features

* **Interaction History:** Easily log and review past conversations, meetups, and meaningful moments.
* **Connection Tracking:** Keep an organized overview of your network to ensure you stay in touch with the people who matter.
* **Clean UI/UX:** A warm, intuitive, and distraction-free interface focused entirely on people over complex data.
* **Fully Responsive:** Beautifully optimized for all devices so you can update your connections anywhere.
